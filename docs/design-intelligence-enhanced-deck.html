Impact

Why It Matters

"Design intelligence produces breakthrough creative work by researching PURPOSE across domains, not just matching categories."
This is the innovation that sets it apart. When you ask "what is the user's brain doing?" instead of "what category is this project?", you discover patterns from unexpected places that feel fresh while being grounded in proven solutions.
The Difference
  • Category-matching: "Healthcare dashboard? Look at healthcare dashboards."
  • Purpose-driven: "Healthcare dashboard needs rapid assessment? Look at trading platforms, medical monitors, gaming HUDs."
  • Result: Designs that feel innovative while solving real cognitive problems.
